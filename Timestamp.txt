'''A timestamp is 3 numbers, a number of hours, minutes and seconds. Given two timestamps, calculate how many seconds is in between them. The moment of the first timestamp occurred before the moment of the second timestamp. '''


h1 = int(input("Enter hours: "))
m1 = int(input("Enter minutes: "))
s1 = int(input("Enter seconds: "))
h2 = int(input("Enter hours: "))
m2 = int(input("Enter minutes: "))
s2 = int(input("Enter seconds: "))
print((h2*3600+m2*60+s2)-(h1*3600+m1*60+s1))
